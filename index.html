J.R.R. Tolkien was an English fantasy author. His work changed the world's vision on fantasy literature forever and (through his son, Christopher Tolkien) we are constantly being amazed by unrelesead books only now coming to the eyes of the public
His most famous books are The Hobbit  and the triology Lord of the Rings , both books got movie adaptations.
